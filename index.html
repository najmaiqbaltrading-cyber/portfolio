About Me

I build things that
mean something.

I'm Abdullah — a 21-year-old Business Administration student at UWC and freelance web developer based in Cape Town. I've been building websites for clients, friends, and people I care about — and I love every bit of it.

My work sits at the intersection of clean code and strong design. I'm not just making things that look good — I want them to feel right to use. Dark mode, glassmorphism, bold typography — that's my comfort zone.

When I'm not in class or building websites, I'm probably working on a new Dear You gift site for someone special, or chasing the next idea that keeps me up at night.
